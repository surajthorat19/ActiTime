Kerala reports India's second Monkeypox case

NEW DELHI: Kerala on Monday reported India's second case of Monkeypox, just three days after it detected the first case. Health minister Veena George said "the second positive case has been confirmed in Kannur district".

The Coronavirus Outbreak

India on Monday reported 16,935 fresh Covid cases, 16,069 recoveries, and 51 deaths. Total active cases has now reached 1,44,264 and the daily positivity rate is 6.48%.
